### The Strategic Framework for Customer Growth, Retention & Net Revenue Expansion

# Executive Summary

Most SaaS companies focus on customer acquisition.

Category leaders focus on customer expansion.

Namaa is designed to grow alongside every service business it serves. While the platform initially solves operational and compliance challenges, its long-term value comes from systematically increasing customer revenue, operational sophistication, and platform adoption over time.

The Namaa Revenue Expansion System defines how customers evolve from a single-product deployment into deeply integrated platform accounts. It establishes the framework for customer success, expansion strategy, stakeholder development, lifecycle automation, and Net Revenue Retention (NRR).

The objective is simple:

> Every successful customer should become larger, more valuable, and more dependent on Namaa each year.

# Strategic Objective

## North Star Metric

**Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**

NRR measures the ability of Namaa to retain and grow existing customer revenue through:

* Product expansion
* Branch expansion
* Payment volume growth
* Additional staff adoption
* Premium feature activation
* Enterprise upgrades

### Long-Term Targets

| Metric                    | Year 1 | Year 3 | Year 5 |
| ------------------------- | ------ | ------ | ------ |
| Gross Revenue Retention   | 90%+   | 95%+   | 96%+   |
| Net Revenue Retention     | 110%+  | 125%+  | 130%+  |
| Multi-Product Adoption    | 35%    | 70%    | 85%    |
| Payment Attachment Rate   | 40%    | 80%    | 90%    |
| Enterprise Expansion Rate | 10%    | 25%    | 40%    |
